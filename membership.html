Why Join ACCIMA?

Grow with a Stronger Business Community

ACCIMA membership connects businesses, entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions to opportunities for networking, advocacy, trade promotion, capacity building, and strategic partnerships.

Member Benefits

What Members Gain

Business Networking

Connect with entrepreneurs, business owners, investors, industry leaders, and decision makers across different sectors.

Advocacy & Representation

Benefit from a collective voice that represents business interests before government, agencies, and policy stakeholders.

Trade Fairs & Exhibitions

Gain access to trade fairs, exhibitions, business forums, and promotional platforms for products and services.

Business Growth Support

Access useful business information, capacity building, training programs, and development opportunities.

Local & Global Linkages

Connect through chamber networks, national associations, and bilateral business relationships.

Credibility & Visibility

Strengthen your business reputation by belonging to a recognized chamber of commerce and business community.

Eligibility

Who Can Become a Member?

ACCIMA membership is open to businesses, professionals, entrepreneurs, associations, and institutions that support the growth of commerce and enterprise.

Registered companies SMEs and startups Manufacturers Traders and merchants Agricultural businesses Service providers Professional groups Investors and institutions
Network & Partnerships

Opportunities Beyond Your Business

Chamber Network

Members become part of a wider chamber movement that supports trade, collaboration, and enterprise development.

Institutional Linkages

ACCIMA provides a platform for engagement with government agencies, development partners, and business institutions.

Bilateral Opportunities

Members may benefit from international business linkages, trade missions, and cross-border partnership opportunities.
