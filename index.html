About ACCIMA

Empowering Businesses & Driving Economic Growth in Aba

The Aba Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (ACCIMA) is a leading voice for businesses in Aba, dedicated to promoting trade, supporting industries, and fostering sustainable economic development.

With decades of excellence, ACCIMA continues to connect entrepreneurs, advocate for favorable business policies, and create opportunities that drive growth across multiple sectors.
