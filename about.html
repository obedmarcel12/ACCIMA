About ACCIMA

Driving Business Growth and Economic Development in Aba

Who We Are

The Aba Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (ACCIMA) is a foremost business membership organization committed to promoting and protecting the interests of the private sector in Aba, Abia State, and Nigeria at large.

Established to serve as the collective voice of the business community, ACCIMA plays a critical role in advancing trade, supporting industrial development, and fostering economic growth across key sectors including commerce, manufacturing, agriculture, and natural resources.

Over the years, the Chamber has built a reputation for excellence by facilitating strategic partnerships, organizing trade fairs and exhibitions, and providing a platform for dialogue between businesses and government. Through advocacy, policy engagement, and capacity building initiatives, ACCIMA continues to create an enabling environment for businesses to thrive.

As a hub for entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders, ACCIMA is dedicated to empowering its members with opportunities, insights, and connections that drive sustainable growth and innovation in the region.

Our Mission

To promote and protect the interests of businesses while fostering sustainable economic growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading voice of commerce and industry in Nigeria and beyond.
