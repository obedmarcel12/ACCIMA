About ACCIMA

The business voice of Aba, promoting commerce, industry, enterprise, and sustainable economic growth.

Who We Are

A Trusted Voice for Business in Aba

The Aba Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (ACCIMA) is a business membership organization representing the interests of companies, entrepreneurs, manufacturers, traders, professionals, and investors in Aba and its surrounding business communities.

ACCIMA works to support enterprise growth, promote trade and investment, encourage industrial development, and strengthen collaboration between the private sector, government institutions, development partners, and the wider chamber movement in Nigeria.

As part of Nigeria’s chamber of commerce network, ACCIMA provides a platform for advocacy, business networking, exhibitions, capacity building, partnerships, and policy engagement aimed at improving the business environment.

Our Background

Rooted in Aba’s Commercial Strength

Aba has long been recognized as one of Nigeria’s most active commercial and industrial cities, known for enterprise, manufacturing, leather works, garments, fabrication, trade, and services.

Public records describe ACCIMA as having come into existence around 1971, giving the Chamber more than five decades of relevance in Aba’s business development story.

ACCIMA is also listed as having registered with the Nigerian Association of Chambers of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture (NACCIMA) on October 25, 1984, connecting the Aba business community to the national chamber movement.

Through the years, ACCIMA has continued to serve as a platform for business advocacy, trade promotion, exhibitions, networking, and private sector engagement in Aba, Abia State, and beyond.

Aba Enterprise

Supporting the Engine Room of Enterprise

Aba’s economy is powered by thousands of entrepreneurs, artisans, manufacturers, traders, and service providers. The city is widely known for leather works, garment production, metal fabrication, repairs, commerce, and small-scale industrial activity.

ACCIMA exists within this energetic business environment to help strengthen private sector participation, encourage collaboration, and create opportunities for businesses to grow beyond local markets.
